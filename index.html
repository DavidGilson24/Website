Solar Atlas is an interactive 3D software targeted to anyone who wants
to learn about the universe, with a focus on our home, the Solar system.

You have the opportunity to interact with each planet of the solar system
and learn facts about each of them and their respective moons.

Educational and immersive, your curiosity and thirst for exploration can be unraveled.

What else is there to see? What else is there to learn?

Solar atlas is developed on unreal engine 5,
with all the 3D models
that are made in blender as well
as the textures, with the help of
nasa's color maps and topography maps.

the music and soundtrack is also made by me,
using fl studio 20.

The project is still in development,
and will be released in april 2024.
